In our society, people do not always turn to a psychologist, so I would like to dispel some doubts here.

The psychologist acts as an independent, neutral side - he does not preach morality, does not condemn, but helps to see the true reasons why we find ourselves in certain situations.

The length of the meetings depends on your individual characteristics and life circumstances. In practice, 2-3 consultations are enough for some, while others need full-fledged psychotherapy and psychoanalysis. In any case, you will have the opportunity to try and make a decision for yourself.

Seeing a psychologist is a bold step in taking care of your own health, because it is about you. It is a professional and loving support and an important contribution for the rest of your life.

Depression, anxiety, fears

Difficulties in defending own boundaries, as well as in expressing thoughts and emotions

Severe crisis situations, loss of a loved one, or a loss in general
